# Front-end Style Guide

## Colors

### Primary

- hsl(243, 86%, 85%)
- hsl(243, 87%, 12%)

### Accent

- hsl(163, 95%, 43%)

### Neutral

- hsl(0, 0%, 50%)

## Typography

### Headings

- Family: [Raleway](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Raleway)
- Weights: 700

### Body

- Family: [Open Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Open+Sans)
- Weights: 400, 700
